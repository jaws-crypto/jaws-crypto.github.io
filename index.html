Advantage of Stellar

By being built on the stellar chain, DEMOCRACY tokens gain many advantages over other DLT tokens

Environmentally Friendly

Stellar's Consensus Protocol (SCP), a form of Federated Byzantine Agreement (FBA), is orders of magnitude more efficient than Proof of Work (PoW) and Proof of Stake (PoS) systems used by most other blockchains.

Secure

The Stellar blockchain is secure against 51% attacks, and cannot undergo a hard fork due to it's powerful consensus mechanism.

Efficient

Stellar transactions are incredibly rapid, with ledgers closing in 3-5 seconds, and each being capable of processing 100,000 opperations (which can increase over time).

Publically audited

Use free open-access sites like stellar.expert to view detailed information about DEMOCRACY tokens, and trustlessly confirm all of the information on this page.

Immutable

All transactions on the stellar blockchain (under Protocol 16) are immutable, so no one can seize your assets or reverse your transactions. Moreover, DEMOCRACY tokens provide immutable authorization. Once your trustline is approved, no one (even us!) can revoke it.

Affordable

With miniscule transaction fees (0.00001 XLM), using DEMOCRACY tokens costs only a tiny fraction of a cent for any transaction.
