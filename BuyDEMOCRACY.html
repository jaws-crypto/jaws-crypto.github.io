How to buy democracy tokens

Once a trustline to democracy token is established, you can buy and sell it with any stellar dex interface (StellarTerm, Lobstr, Interstellar, StellarX, Stellar Laboratory), just like any other stellar asset. The only difference is that you must establish a capped trustline, which many programs/interfaces are incapable of. As such, three options are given below to establish a valid, capped trustline.

With Solar Wallet

Follow the steps below to establish a trustline with Solar Wallet

Steps:

  1. Click on your account
  2. Click the menu in the top right (three horizontal white bars)
  3. Select "Assets & Balances"
  4. Click "+ Add Asset To Your Account" (at the top of the page)
  5. Select "Add Custom Asset"
  6. Enter "DEMOCRACY" in the "CODE" field, "GDEMOYMTQQ4ZHWVJFIQPORWGQYV3BXQKIRWZ7X4ETB5VZTON3BBNLETI" in the "ISSUER" field, and any number less than or equal to 1,000,000 in the "LIMIT" field.
  7. A button for "Trust" will appear. Click it.
  8. Enter your account password, and press "CONFIRM" (at the bottom of the page)

Using Stellar Laboratory

To create the trustline quickly using Stellar Laboratory, enter your public key/adress in the field below. This will link you to a completed XDR (transaction) on Stellar Laboratory. Simply enter your private key on Stellar Laboratory to sign the transaction, and submit it.

Directly (Not recommended)

Alternatively, enter your account secret key in the box below, and we will securely execute a transaction to establish an appropriate trustline. Please note that your secret key can be used to execute any transaction for your account. Using one of the two options mentioned above is more secure.
